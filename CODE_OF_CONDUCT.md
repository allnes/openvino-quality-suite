# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

OVIQS contributors are expected to keep project communication respectful,
specific and focused on technical work.

Examples of expected behavior:

- use welcoming and inclusive language;
- give constructive, evidence-backed feedback;
- respect differing viewpoints and experience levels;
- focus criticism on code, documentation, tests and process.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults or personal attacks;
- publishing private information without explicit permission;
- disruptive behavior that prevents project work from continuing.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove unacceptable content and restrict participation when
needed to keep the project safe and productive. Security-sensitive reports
should follow `SECURITY.md` instead of public issue discussion.
